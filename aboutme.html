About Me

My passion for technology started young when I first built and coded robots with Lego Mindstorms in the FIRST Lego League. That hands-on experience sparked my curiosity and laid the groundwork for my love of problem-solving, creativity, and innovation through technology.

Over time, my interests have expanded to web development, digital marketing, design, and editing. I enjoy creating visually compelling projects, building intuitive websites, and exploring how strategy and creativity intersect in the digital world.

I’m currently pursuing my degree, set to graduate in May 2025, and am actively growing my skills to prepare for a dynamic career in tech. Whether it’s developing user-friendly websites, contributing to IT solutions, or crafting unique digital marketing campaigns, I’m driven to make an impact in this ever-evolving field.

Beyond technology, I’m also a passionate cellist and a lover of orchestral music—bringing a creative and thoughtful approach to everything I do.

This portfolio showcases my journey, skills, and projects as I carve out my place in the tech world. Let’s build something amazing together!
